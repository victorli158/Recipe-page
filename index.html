Chicken Adobo

Description

Chicken adobo is a dish that involves a soy sauce and vinegar marinade.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Dark soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Black peppercorns
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Bay leaves
  • Brown sugar

Directions

  1. Marinate your chicken thighs overnight in all of the above ingredients except for the bay leaves
  2. Fry your chicken thighs on medium heat until sauce is bubbling
  3. Add the bay leaves and a half cup of water and simmer on low heat, covered, for 10 minutes.
  4. Flip your chicken thighs once and cover them again, simmering for another 10 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and enjoy!
